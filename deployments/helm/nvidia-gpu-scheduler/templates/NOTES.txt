Try to manage gpu info with the following apis from the k8s kube-apiserver:
  1.monitoring gpu pod。
    kubectl get --raw "/apis/metrics.nvidia.com/v1alpha1/podresources"
    kubectl get --raw “/apis/metrics.nvidia.com/v1alpha1/podresources?watch=true”
  2.monitoring gpu node.
    kubectl get --raw "/apis/metrics.nvidia.com/v1alpha1/gpuinfos"
